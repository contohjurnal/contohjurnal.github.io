9. tuliskan contoh descriptive text berjudul my dream


║DESCRIPTIVE TEXT║

Answer

Everybody has a dream. A dream about the future or the immediate need is everyone's wish.  I have a dream for my future. It is a best way to be a pharmacist who has responsibility of caring the medicine. A pharmacist is not only responsible for the medicine but also the patients who need the best advice for their health.

My parents always support my dream. Both of them have prepared all the things to actualize my dream, to be a pharmacist. My mother always gives the good advice to succeed in achieving my dream. My mother always says," Achieving a dream is not about how much the finances we have but it needs a confidence and loyalty in our heart."

Explanation

DESCRIPTIVE TEXTGenre teks yang menjabarkan suatu benda/orang/tempat dan hal lainnya secara spesifik.

SOCIAL FUNCTION/COMMUNICATIVE PURPOSE to describe people/place/things specifically ( untuk mendeskripsikan orang/tempat/hal -hal secara khusus )

GENERIC STRUCTURE

{ Struktural teks deskripsi }

1.  Identification  

Bagian paragraf ini  terletak di awal sebagai pengenalan siapa/apa yang akan dijabarkan dalam teks

2. Description

Bagian paragraf selanjutnya menjabarkan ciri khas-nya bagaimana dari orang/benda/tempat/hal yang di bicarakan.

LANGUAGE FEATURES

{ Unsur - unsur kebahasaan yang digunakan }

1. Penggunaan kekhususan hal-hal yang dilibatkan dalam teks nya. { Specific participant }

Example : my dream, my house, mount merapi

2. Penggunaan  kata sifat ( adjective )

Kata sifat  digunakan dalam penjabaran karakter khusus dari hal yang dibicarakan.

3. Penggunaan pola simple present tense

Dikarenakan sifat informasi nya berupa fakta, maka menggunakan pola simple present tense.

4. Penggunaan kata kerja aksi

Kata kerja aksi memuat informasi tindakan.

12. contoh describing tentang my best friend


Describing my best friend:

She was tall, brown skin, curly tied up hair. Her smile is charming with pure white teeth. Her name is Susan. She's my best friend. We've been friends since elementary school. Our houses are close together so we always play together every day. Susan's house is very close to mine, only a few houses apart.

Susan loves to draw. Her hands are very skilled in drawing anything. Susan can draw landscapes, draw people, draw animals, and she can even draw me.

Susan has a very cute little brother, he is four years old. We always play together. Susan loves and cares for her brother very much.

Susan is also very fond of books. She has a lot of books in her room. There are comic books, short story books, novels, and other science books. Susan always showed me the new book she got from her father and told me about its contents.

Susan really likes meatballs. I also like meatballs. On holidays we always eat meatballs together at our favorite restaurant. Susan loves spicy meatballs and eats a lot of them.

Pembahasan

Describing someone adalah kamu menceritakan dan mendeskripsikan seseorang, misalnya temanmu. Kamu ceritakan aspek fisik, nama, hobinya apa, rumahnya dimana, sejak kapan kalian bersahabat, dan juga keluarganya.

Kamu bisa memakai cara ini: kamu tulis dulu deskripsi tersebut dalam bahasa Indonesia lalu kamu terjemahkan ke dalam bahasa Inggris dengan bantuan kamus bahasa Inggris. Dengan banyak menulis maka nantinya kamu akan mudah membuat tulisan langsung dalam bahasa Inggris.
