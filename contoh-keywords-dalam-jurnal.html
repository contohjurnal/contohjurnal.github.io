7. B. Words in Context: Identifying a Part of SpeechLook at the keywords below, and identify the part of speech from those words:Keywords Part of Speech1. Admission : __________________2. And : __________________3. College : __________________4. Contribute : __________________5. Popular : __________________6. Heritage : __________________7. Extracurricular : __________________8. They : __________________9. Ask : __________________10. Question : __________________​


Jawaban:

Keywords Part of Speech

1. Admission : Noun

2. And : Conjunction

3. College : Noun

4. Contribute : Verb

5. Popular : Adjective

6. Heritage : Noun

7. Extracurricular : Noun

8. They : Pronoun

9. Ask : Verb

10. Question : Noun

Penjelasan:

There are eight parts of speech in the English language: noun, pronoun, verb, adjective, adverb, preposition, conjunction, and interjection. The part of speech indicates how the word functions in meaning as well as grammatically within the sentence. An individual word can function as more than one part of speech when used in different circumstances. Understanding parts of speech is essential for determining the correct definition of a word when using the dictionary.
