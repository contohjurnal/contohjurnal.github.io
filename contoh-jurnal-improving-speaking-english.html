3. speaking english tentang liburan​


Jawaban:

Last year in December my family and I went to Singapore and Malaysia. First we went to the zoo and we saw lots of monkeys and animals. We also went to the water park, it was very fun because there were buckets of water splashing everywhere and my sister and I got really wet. The next day my mum and dad took us to Universal Studios at Sentosa Island. It was so big with lots of things to do. My favorite ride was the 3D transformers ride.

In Malaysia, we went to visit my cousins and stayed at their house for a few days. My cousins had a big double storey house with lots of chickens, roosters and even a swimming pool. We did lots of shopping and eating. My favorite food was Burger King and Prata. My favorite drink was called Bandung, it is a cold rose milk drink in ice. It was so yum. My holiday was so much fun and I can’t wait to go there again.

Translate :

Tahun lalu di bulan Desember aku dan keluarga pergi ke Singapura dan Malaysia. Pertama kami pergi ke kebun binatang dan kami melihat banyak monyet dan binatang. Kami juga pergi ke taman air, itu sangat menyenangkan karena ada ember air yang memercik ke mana-mana dan saya dan saudara perempuan ku basah kuyup. Keesokan harinya ibu dan ayah ku membawa kami ke Universal Studios di Pulau Sentosa. Itu sangat besar dengan banyak hal yang harus dilakukan. Perjalanan favorit ku adalah perjalanan transformer 3D.

Di Malaysia, kami pergi mengunjungi sepupu ku dan tinggal di rumah mereka selama beberapa hari. Sepupu ku memiliki rumah dua lantai yang besar dengan banyak ayam, ayam jantan dan bahkan kolam renang. Kami melakukan banyak belanja dan makan. Makanan favorit saya adalah Burger King dan Prata. Minuman favorit ku bernama Bandung, itu adalah minuman susu mawar dingin dalam es. Itu sangat enak. Liburan ini sangat menyenangkan dan aku tidak sabar untuk pergi ke sana lagi.

Materi: General translation, sign sentence
Kata kunci: translation, writing

Perlu dijelaskan lebih detail, apakah kita harus mengartikan kalimat diatas ke Bahasa Indonesia, atau memang harus menjelaskan maksud dari dituliskannya kalimat tersebut. 7. Tolong buatkan hartatory exposition tentang The Importance of Speaking English


IMPORTANCE OF SPEAKING ENGLISH

English nowadays has an important role in our daily life. It is the massive means of communication. Is there a strong argument which says that English will not give any benefit?

Learning to speak English well may be the best way to improve our life. It seems all the people in the world have agreed to use English to talk to each other. About 1,500,000,000 people in the world speak English. While another 1,000,000,000 are still learning English.

If we can communicate in English, we can contact people from all over the world. We can talk about our ideas and opinions on Internet discussion groups. We can chat with other interesting people to learn about their life and culture.

If we can communicate in English, we can travel more easily. English is spoken in more than 100 countries. If we lost, we can ask directions or ask for help. Who knows, English will save our life  somewhere someday.

That is why we should make every effort possible to find somebody to speak with. Where can we find people who can speak English with us? We can find them at school, shopping mall, tourist destination, etc.

Above all, don’t be afraid to speak English. We must try to speak, even if we make mistakes. We cannot learn without mistakes. So, speak English as much as possible! How to do that? Below is the simple tips to practice speaking English
1. Find friends to practice speaking English. They are:
2. If you are too shy to speak English with friends, try to stand in front of a mirror. You will see yourself then it’s time to speak with yourself
3. If speaking to yourself is still hard to do, then just simply speaking English loudly. Don’t think to whom,  just easily speak up. It is easy, isn’t it?
