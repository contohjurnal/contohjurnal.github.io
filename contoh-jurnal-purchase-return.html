1. Sebutkan pengaruh purchase return dalam pencatatan akuntansi


Purchase Return / Retur Pembelian merupakan pengembalian barang yang sudah dibeli karena cacat dam sebagainya.

Proses pencatatan retur pembelian:
Utang / Kas (D)
Retur pembelian (K)
Mengapa bisa begitu?
1. Transaksi pembelian perkas / kredit
Jurnalnya:
Pembelian (D)
Kas / Utang (K)
Karena pembelian akan mengurangi kas, maka di kredit.
Karena pembelian akan menambah utang, maka di kredit.
2. Transaksi retur pembelian
Kas / Utang (D)
Retur pembelian (K)
Karena retur pembelian akan menambah kas, maka di debit.
Karena pembelian akan mengurangi utang, maka di debit.

Jadi transaksi retur pembelian akan berpengaruh terhadap utang / kas, tergantung cara yang dipakai dalam pembelian tersebut.

7. cara mencatat return pembelian di jurnal umum​


Jawaban:

Terlampir pada penjelasan.

Penjelasan:

Materi : Akuntansi Dasar - Jurnal Umum

Retur pembelian merupakan pengembalian barang dagang dari pembeli kepada penjual (dari sisi pembeli). Sehingga ada dua metode pencatatan jurnal nya (secara periodik dan perpetual)

Contoh :

Tanggal 23 Agustus 2022, dikembalikan barang dagang yang dibeli dari PT Samsu sebesar 450.000

Jurnal retur pembelian (periodik) adalah :

(D) Hutang Dagang  450.000

(K) Retur Pembelian                 450.000

Sedangkan apabila menggunakan perpetual, maka :

(D) Hutang Dagang                        450.000

(K) Persediaan Barang Dagangan                 450.000

(karena dalam metode perpetual, menggunakan kartu persediaan dalam mengendalikan persediaan, sehingga mengurangi persediaan secara langsung)

Rujukan : Financial Accounting 4th IFRS edition (2019), Weygandt, Kimmel, Kieso. Wiley.

34. Contoh medical form , purchase form , order form, feservation form ?


Answer:

1. Medical form

Medical form is a form which is filled in by a patient to a doctor. This form contains the patient's personal health information, such as his/her illness, allergies, currently taken medicines, family health history, etc.

2. Purchase form

Purchase form is also known as purchase order (PO) form. Purchase form is a commercial document which is made by a buyer to a seller and used as a request to purchase products or services (normally in large quantities). This form contains The company name, client's information, types, quantities, and agreed prices of products or service purchased.

3. Order form

Order form is a commercial document which is filled in by customers and used to order goods from a company. This form contains The company name, client's information, types, quantities, and agreed prices of products ordered.

4. Reservation form

Reservation form is a form which is filled in when booking or reserving something. it can be a hotel room, entertainment tickets, transportation, etc. This form contains the client's information, a number of things or places booked, and the date and time of when it's used. Some reservation forms require down payment as a reservation deal.
