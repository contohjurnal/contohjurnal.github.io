12. deskripsi tentang pengusaha,dosen dan perancang dalam bahasa inggris


Lecturer. (dosen)

A lecturer is, in the broadest sense, a person who gives lectures or other public speeches. 

In the United Kingdom and Ireland, a lecturer holds an open-ended, tenure-track or tenured position at a university or similar institution, and is often an academic at an early career stage who teaches, conducts research, and leads research groups. 

Most lecturers typically hold permanent contracts at their academic institution. In terms of responsibilities and recognition, the position of an open-ended lecturer on a permanent contract is equivalent to Assistant Professor or Associate Professor in the North American academic system. This is a tenure-track or tenured position, although UK tenure has eroded since 1988.

In North America (United States, Canada) and other countries, the term lecturer generally denotes an academic expert without tenure in the university, who is hired to teach on a full- or part-time basis, but who is not paid to conduct research. In most research universities in the United States, the title of lecturer requires a doctorate or equivalent degree.

In the Church of England, the position of lecturer can also refer to a junior clergyman.

Entrepreneur. (pengusaha)

Someone who exercises initiative by organizing aventure to take benefit of an opportunity and, as the decision maker, decides what, how,and how much of a good or service will be produced.

An entrepreneur supplies risk capital as a risk taker, and monitors and controls the business activities. 

The entrepreneur is usually a sole proprietor, a partner, or the one who owns the majority of shares in an incorporated venture.

Designer. (perancang)

A designer is a person who designs. More formally, a designer is an agent that "specifies the structural properties of a designobject". In practice, anyone who creates tangible or intangible objects, such as consumer products, processes, laws, games, graphics, services and experiences is referred to as a designer.

33. Bahasa inggrisnya dosen pembimbing apa ya?


Dosen termasuk salah satu sebutan untuk seorang guru, namun dosen diperuntukkan bagi guru yang mengajar di Universitas, adapun dosen pembimbing dalam bahasa Inggris lebih tepat disebut sebagai "supervisor"

Pembahasan

ada banyak sekali sebutan dalam bahasa inggris bagi dosen, di antaranya adalah; college teacher, lecturer, teacher, doctor, professor, dan lain lain

sementara kata pembimbing lebih cenderung pada kata "supervisor" dikarenakan seorang pembimbing selalu mengawasi orang yang di bawah bimbingannya.

contoh kata supervisor sebagai arti dosen pembimbing di dalam suatu kalimat adalah:

who is your supervisor? (siapa dosen pembimbingmu?)my supervisor is very wise man (dosen pembimbingku adalah orang yang sangat bijak)whenever you need help, come to your supervisor (kapanpun kamu butuh bantuan, datangi dosen pembimbingmu)Pelajari Lebih Lanjut
