v = 96 km/jam

t = 210 menit

s = ... km

__

t = 210 menit ÷ 60 menit

t = 3,5 jam

s = v . t

s = 96 km/jam . 3,5 jam

s = 96 . 3,5

s = 336 km.

Penyelesaian ☁️

t = 210 menit : 60 menit

t = 3,5 jam

maka,

S = V × t

S = 96 km/jam × 3,5 jam

S = 336 km
