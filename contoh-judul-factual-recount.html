6. explain the function of personal,imaginaive,factual recount?



An imaginative recount is the re-telling of events, usually in the first person. The imaginative part allows for embellishment beyond mere facts - perfect for primary school writing.

A simple idea would be to ask students to recount their journey to school. If you require more of the imaginative component, give them an element (or series of elements) they have to include. It can be fun to give these out as cards selected from a pack. Ideas here could involve including the following -

a thunderstorm, an old lady, a wild horse, a can of lemonade, a giant insect etc, etc.

If you require a stimulus for recount ideas in this semi-autobiographical way, Laurie Lee's first day at school has a nice balance of embellishment and recollection in 'Cider With Rosie'.

13. Explain the function of personal,imagenative,factual recount?


# Function: to tell past events for the purpose of informing or entertaining. Events are usually aranged in a
temporal sequence.
#. Types of recount:
a. Personal recount : retelling of an activity that the writer/speaker has been personally involve in, such as:
biography, autobiography
b. Factual recount : recording the particular of an accident, such as : report of a scientific experiment,
police report , news report, historical account
c. Imaginative recount : talking on an imaginary role and giving detail of events.

14. perbedaan dari personal ricount, biographical recount, Factual recount, imaginative recount


Personal Recount: Jenis recount text yang pertama ini biasanya berupa informasi atau cerita pribadi yang merupakan pengalaman dari si penulis recount text itu sendiri.

Factual Recount: Jenis recount text yang kedua ini biasanya berisi mengenai laporan dari sebuah peristiwa atau kejadian nyata yang benar-benar telah terjadi dimasa lampau.

Imaginative Recount: Jenis recount text selanjutnya Imaginative, yaitu sebuah recount yang mana hanyalah sebuah cerita imajinasi dari si-penulis. Namun, cerita imajinasi tersebut masih tetap merupakan sebuah peristiwa yang telah terjadi di masa lampau.

Biographical: Jenis recount text yang terakhir adalah biographical dimana text ini menceritakan riwayat, kisah atau history hidup dari seseorang. Namun, pada penulisannya kita menggunakan sudut pandang orang ketiga, yaitu: she, he, dan it.

Tuesday, September 30th, 2008  
It was takbiran night. It was also my birthday, and nothing happened. Or I had thought nothing would happen, but I was wrong.   That night, I was watching television with my family when I heard someone lit fireworks in my front yard. I peeped trough my window glass but could see nothing. It was very dark outside. Then I thought it had to be my cousins who lit the fireworks. Then I plopped down on my sofa again and tried to concentrate on the television since my mind raced with disappointment that no one gave something special on my birthday. I shrugged, it was almost the end of the day and I became pessimist. Five minutes later my mobile beeped. It was a text from my friend asking me to come out. Wondering what was going on, I grabbed my jacket and hurried to the front door. I was surprised to see her bring a bag full of firework and fire drills. Next I was surprised to see my other friend come out from the darkness. She brought a beautiful birthday cake on her hands. Oh my God! I shrieked. Then they gave the cake to me who was too stunned to say anything. I realized I was blushing furiously because my whole family was watching. Not to mention my neighbors too!   A plain day, or I thought it was before, turned out to be one of the greatest moment in my life. I didn’t even know how to describe what I felt. Happy was the simplest word.

20. contoh factual recount tentang kecelakaan di daerah wonogiri beserta terjemahannya​


bahasa Indonesia

Pada tanggal 15 Mei 2023, terjadi kecelakaan di daerah Wonogiri yang melibatkan dua kendaraan. Kejadian tersebut terjadi sekitar pukul 08.30 pagi di Jalan Raya Wonogiri-Klaten.

Kedua kendaraan yang terlibat adalah sebuah mobil sedan warna hitam dan sebuah truk box. Menurut saksi mata, mobil sedan tampak melaju dengan kecepatan tinggi ketika tiba-tiba truk box muncul dari samping dan menabrak bagian depan mobil sedan.

Tabrakan tersebut mengakibatkan mobil sedan mengalami kerusakan parah di bagian depannya. Sementara itu, truk box terguling ke sisi jalan dan menghambat lalu lintas di sekitar lokasi kejadian.

Tim medis dan petugas kepolisian segera dikerahkan ke tempat kejadian untuk memberikan pertolongan kepada korban dan mengatur lalu lintas. Beberapa korban luka-luka dievakuasi ke rumah sakit terdekat untuk mendapatkan perawatan medis.

Penyebab pasti kecelakaan tersebut masih dalam penyelidikan oleh pihak berwenang. Namun, beberapa saksi mata menyebutkan bahwa kemungkinan terjadinya kesalahan pengemudi dalam menjaga jarak dan kecepatan kendaraan.

bahasa inggris

On May 15, 2023, an accident occurred in the Wonogiri area involving two vehicles. The incident took place around 8:30 in the morning on the Wonogiri-Klaten Highway.

The two vehicles involved were a black sedan and a box truck. According to eyewitnesses, the sedan was seen speeding when suddenly the box truck appeared from the side and collided with the front of the sedan.

The collision resulted in severe damage to the front of the sedan. Meanwhile, the box truck overturned on the side of the road, obstructing traffic around the accident site.

Medical teams and police officers were immediately dispatched to the scene to provide assistance to the victims and manage traffic. Several injured victims were evacuated to the nearest hospital for medical treatment.

The exact cause of the accident is still under investigation by the authorities. However, some eyewitnesses mentioned the possibility of driver error in maintaining distance and vehicle speed.

25. perbedaan dari personal ricount, biographical recount, Factual recount, imaginative recount


Personal Recount: Jenis recount text yang pertama ini biasanya berupa informasi atau cerita pribadi yang merupakan pengalaman dari si penulis recount text itu sendiri.

Factual Recount: Jenis recount text yang kedua ini biasanya berisi mengenai laporan dari sebuah peristiwa atau kejadian nyata yang benar-benar telah terjadi dimasa lampau.

Imaginative Recount: Jenis recount text selanjutnya Imaginative, yaitu sebuah recount yang mana hanyalah sebuah cerita imajinasi dari si-penulis. Namun, cerita imajinasi tersebut masih tetap merupakan sebuah peristiwa yang telah terjadi di masa lampau.

Biographical: Jenis recount text yang terakhir adalah biographical dimana text ini menceritakan riwayat, kisah atau history hidup dari seseorang. Namun, pada penulisannya kita menggunakan sudut pandang orang ketiga, yaitu: she, he, dan it.

35. explain the function of personal ,imaginative,factual recount?


Personal: To retell an event that the writer was probably involved in.

Imaginative: The author writes an imaginary role and gives details in the recount. The function is to entertain readers. (e.g. A Day in The Life of A Pirate, Story of A Mango Tree)

Factual: To record an incident that happened. (e.g. a science experiment, a police report)
