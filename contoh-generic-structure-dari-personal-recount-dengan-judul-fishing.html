6. 1 contoh text recount dan berilah generic structure nya dari teks tersebut​


My Holiday In Bali

ORIENTATION

When i was in 4th grade, mu family and i had a vacation in Bali. We stayed there for one week. It was amazong and full of impressive experiences during the vacation.

Paragraf 2 - 6 = EVENTS

On the first day in Bali, we visited Kuta beach. Then, we moved to the hotel and checked - in. We took a break and had some meals before continuing the trip. After prepared ourselves, we went to Kuta Beach again. There were so many tourists that spent their holiday in Bali.

The second day, our next destination was Tanjung Benoa beach. We really enjoyed our time there, because there were so many watersports. Such as jet ski, snorkeling, banana boat, etc. We also visited Penyu island.

The third day there, we prepared ourselves & we went to Tanah Lot. We enjoyed the view by walking around while waiting for the amazing sunset.

(Untuk paragraf selanjutnya, lihat digambar ya!)

PARAGRAF 7 - 8 = REORIENTATION

PENJELASAN:

GENERIC STRUCTURES

• Orientation
Memberikan informasi tentang siapa, di mana, dan kapan peristiwa atau kegiatan terjadi di masa lampau.

• Events
Rekaman peristiwa yang terjadi, biasanya disampaikan dalam urutan kronologis. Seperti "in the first day. I ... . And in the next day ... . And in the last day ..."

Pada bagian events juga biasanya terdapat komentar pribadi tentang peristiwa atau kejadian yang diceritakan.

• Reorientation
Terdapat pengulangan pengenalan yang ada di orientation, pengulangan yang merangkum rentetan peristiwa, kejadian atau kegiatan yang diceritakan.

My First Trip to Pari Island

Last September, I went to Pari Island for a holiday. I went there with Sarah, Andi, Eveline, and Yoko. That was my first time to travel there.

Our boat departed from Muara Angke port to the island at 8.00 AM. We arrived at Pari Island around 10.00 Am. After that, we went to our guest house that we had booked before. Fortunately, our guest house was located near to the port, so we did not have to walk so far. The first thing that we did after that was rent bicycle. It only cost IDR 20.000 per day. Then we did cycling for a while and had lunch before we decided to do snorkeling.

When we went snorkeling, the fisherman who became our guide brought us to the beach far enough from our guest house. We did snorkeling there. The water was very clear. We could see groups of tiny fishes and beautiful coral under the sea. After we did snorkeling for approximately three hours, we returned to our guest house. We ended up the first day by getting dinner and took some walks around.

On the second day, we woke up so early to watch sunrise. We did not need to go far to watch the sunrise because our guest house was so near to the beach and we could watch the sunrise there. Sunrise on the beach, what a beautiful view!

After we spent long enough time to watch the sunrise, we decided to get breakfast. There was a festival at that time. We went to the festival and had breakfast there. We ate some delicious sea foods. We felt time went so fast that day. We needed to return to our guest house because our boat that would bring us home would depart at 10.00 AM.

In summary, my first trip to Pari Island was so enjoyable. I would like to visit this place again together with my family in another chance.

Kami bisa melihat ikan-ikan kecil dan karang yang indah di bawah laut. Setelah kami snorkeling selama kurang lebih tiga jam, kami kembali ke rumah penginapan. Setelah menghabiskan waktu cukup lama untuk menyaksikan matahari terbit, kami memutuskan untuk sarapan. Ada sebuah festival pada waktu itu. Kami pergi ke festival dan sarapan di sana. Kami makan beberapa makanan laut yang lezat. Kami merasa waktu berjalan sangat cepat hari itu. Kami harus kembali ke rumah penginapan karena kapal kami yang akan membawa kami pulang akan berangkat pukul 10.00.

Singkatnya, perjalanan pertamaku ke Pulau Pari begitu menyenangkan. Aku ingin mengunjungi tempat ini lagi bersama keluargaku di kesempatan lain.

Ciri-ciri Recount Teks:

1. Menggunakan kalimat Past Tense (lampau), seperti went, departed, would, woke up, dan lain sebagainya.

2. Menggunakan adverb dan adverbial phrase seperti: last September, Pari Island, on then second day, dan lain sebagainya.

3. Menggunakan conjunction dan time connectives seperti: and, before, then, after that, dan lain sebagainya

Depart from Muara Angke → Arrived → Went to the guest house → Rent bicycle and cycling →  Had lunch →  Snorkeling →  dan seterusnya.

24. generic structure of recount text ?


Generic Structure dari Recount Text: Orientation – Menyediakan pembaca mengenai latar belakang atau informasi yang diperlukan untuk memahami suatu teks. Contohnya pengenalan tentang tokoh, waktu, dan peristiwa utama yang akan terjadi. Sequence of events – Menyediakan rangkaian peristiwa di dalam cerita yang ada di teks tersebut. Contohnya Ada bercerita tentang liburan ke pantai, maka di tahap sequence of events, Anda bercerita kegiatan-kegiatan yang dilakukan di pantai mulai dari sampai di pantai hingga pulang ke rumah. Re-orientation – Memberikan sebuah ringkasan komentar, dan penyataan mengenai peristiwa yang telah terjadi. Misalnya setelah Anda bercerita tentang liburan ke pantai, Anda merasa begitu gembira, sehingga Anda mengatakan di akhir cerita, bahwa Anda begitu senang serta bahagia karena dapat berkunjung ke pantai tersebut. Referensi: Department for Education of South Australia. 2012. 1. Orientation
   Introduce the participant, time and place of the events
2. Sequence of events
   The happening of events
3. Re-orientation
   The conclusion of events

31. generic structure of procedural recount


GENERIC STRUCTURE RECOUNT TEXT

Orientation : An introduction that provides the setting and introduce participant. Pada bagian orientation awal inilah kita akan mengenalkan materi cerita kita beserta siapa kah yang terlibat dalam cerita ini.

Events : Telling the event in a sequence (Event 1, Event 2, Event 3, and so on). Dalam bagian inilah kita akan merangkai cerita dengan menceritakan kajadian – kejadian secara berurutan.

Reorientation : Optional, containing conclusion of the story. Bagian ini biasanya bersifat optional, yang artinya kita bisa memilih untuk tidak memakainya dalam cerita kita, jika dipakai, maka bagian ini akan berisi kesimpulan cerita dari recount text yang kita buat.

33. Apa saja language feature dan generic structure dari recount text?


 Recount Text

Generic structure :
1. Orientation : introduction of the participants, setting of place, setting of time
2. Events : chronological order of the events being told
3. Reorientation : comments by the writer, optional.

Language feature
1. in the first person POV
2. Using first person pronouns (I, me, my, etc)
2. Using the past tense
3. Using time connectives (e.g. firstly, next, then, after that, etc)

36. Tulis 1 contoh Recount text beserta generic structure nya dan penjelasannya​


Recount text adalah teks yang menceritakan tentang suatu cerita, tindakan, atau menceritakan suatu kejadian atau pengalaman yang terjadi dimasa lalu contohnya seperti pengalaman berlibur atau

structure

Orientation: memceritakan mengenai latarbelakang tentang siapa, di mana, kapan kejadian terjadi.

Events: menceritakan suatu peristiwa yang terjadi setelahnya diceritakan.

Reorientation: berisi rangkuman atau puncak dari semua kejadian, berisi pendapat atau kesan penulis tentang kejadian yang diceritakan.

Last week, me and my friends went to the mountain after collage. We used public train. We reached the beach at 10 a.m. The mountain was beautiful and clean.

Events:

At the mountain, it was fun. We take a lots of picture. After that, we built a tend for the night. In the night, we made bonfire together. We roasted fishes that we brought. In the midnight, it was time to share our scary stories. One by one, we told our story.

Reorientation:

At the morning, we went home. It was a moment i never forgot.
